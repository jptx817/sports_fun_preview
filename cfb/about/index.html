About

CFB Reporting Stack

A local, static decision-support surface for the completed 2026 preseason CFB reporting artifacts.

What The Model Is

The CFB model is a saved preseason and weekly reporting stack built around team ratings, game projections, totals context, matchup narratives, team capsules, and coverage diagnostics.

What This Dashboard Shows

The dashboard presents preserved reporting artifacts as readable pages: Week 1 slate context, matchup cards, team identity capsules, and entity coverage diagnostics.

Market Comparison Pending

Market Comparison Pending means real saved Week 1 betting market lines are not available in the reporting stack. The dashboard displays placeholders and does not estimate sportsbook lines.

Recommendations Disabled

Recommendations are intentionally disabled because model-vs-market comparison is not structurally ready without real refreshed markets. This surface is not an action engine.

FCS Placeholders

FCS placeholders identify opponents that have limited saved profile coverage. They are expected limitations, not automatic model errors.

When Live Markets Are Available

Once real markets are saved and validated, the weekly report can show true model-vs-market comparison status while still preserving recommendation gating.

Decision Support

This is decision-support reporting, not automated betting advice.
